Profiling Health Equity in England:
How does health and deprivation vary spatially?

This analysis explores the relationship between number of deaths and socio-economic influences, seeking to figure out whether some socio-economic influences are linked with certain causes of death. Additionally, the number of years lost (as a result of early death) has been explored. This research seeks to contribute existing literature and research regarding the relationship between socio-economic conditions and health in deprived areas.
