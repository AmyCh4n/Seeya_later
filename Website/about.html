About Us

We are a team of spatial data analysts from all over the world who are united by a shared interest in health-related issues. We are all studying the MSc Smart Cities and Urban Analytics at the Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis (CASA), Bartlett Faculty of the Built Environment, University College London (UCL). This website is a part of a project that we were asked to design in the module of Spatial Data Capture, Storage and Analysis.
