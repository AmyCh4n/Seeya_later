Story: An outline of the project's methodology and key findings.To understand the transportation characteristics across England and Wales, we used a clustering methodology to identify different groups of transport profiles. This analysis has been performed at the MSOA level given that this is the lowest level of geographic aggregation for which data was available. Subsequently, a classification analysis was performed to understand what demographic factors are related to these profiles.
